Joshua's Banana Shake

Contents:

  1. Ingredients
  2. Instructions
  3. Sources

Ingredients

Ingredients Quantity
Banana 1
Milk 1 cup
Vanilla Extract 1/4 tsp
Cinnamon 1/8 tsp
Lemon Juice 1 tsp
Vanilla Ice Cream 3 fat scoops

For Serving

  • Whipped Cream
  • Dried Banana

Instructions

  1. To the bottom of your blender (a highspeed blender is my first choice, but any blender will work here), add the sliced banana.
  2. Next, pour in the milk, vanilla, cinnamon and lemon juice.
  3. Add the three big scoops of ice cream to the blender last.
  4. Blend everything together until very smooth and creamy. If itâ€™s too cold and not blending, add small splashes of milk until the banana milkshake liquifies and is pourable.
